“I Will Always Be Me” - Dell Technologies and Intel

This project is recommended to us by our primary contact, Prof. Mohamedally.

"I Will Always Be Me" is a book created for individuals living with motor neurone disease (MND) and their loved ones. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining one's identity and voice amidst the challenges of MND. The book aims to empower individuals to preserve their voice through voice banking technology, which allows them to record their voice and use it later with assistive speech devices. It highlights the impact of MND on speech and identity, and the role of technology, particularly the partnership between Dell Technologies and Intel, in supporting individuals with MND.

Main Features of “I Will Always Be Me”

  • Voice Banking: The project allows individuals experiencing difficulty speaking due to MND to bank their voice. By recording their voice while reading the provided book, individuals can create a digital version of their voice.
  • Digital Voice Usage: Once the digital voice is created, individuals can use it through various devices such as computers, tablets, or smartphones. This enables them to communicate in their own voice, maintaining their identity and sense of self-expression.
  • Accessibility: The initiative emphasizes accessibility by providing a user-friendly process that can be done from the comfort of one's home. Additionally, it accommodates individuals with different physical abilities, allowing for voice banking even if fine motor skills are limited.

What have we learnt?

1) User-Centric Software Design

2) Aesthetic User Interface Design

3) Simplicity and Accessibility

1) User-Centric Software Design

"I Will Always Be Me" puts strong emphasis on helping those with MND. It prioritizes user needs, offering tailored content for people with MND and their caregivers. The book demonstrates a commitment to addressing MND challenges through innovative solutions and empathetic storytelling.[1]

We are inspired to do the same and create software that can truly help them. Therefore we put a lot of effort into user requirement research in order to learn about what our target users need the most.[2]

Learn more about our process here.

2) Aesthetic User Interface Design

"I Will Always Be Me" incorporates rounded buttons and curved elements, maintaining a consistent art style with attractive illustrations. This design approach ensures a visually pleasing and user-friendly experience, facilitating their engagement with the material and fostering a deeper connection with the narrative.[3]

Anima V3's user interface design is heavily inspired by "I Will Always Be Me". We love how their design is very aesthetically pleasing, and we learnt that this is important in order to attract users to use our product.[4]

Learn more about our design choices here.

3) Simplicity and Accessibility

"I Will Always Be Me" provides a straightforward and accessible solution to preserve one's voice. Through a simple process of reading the book aloud, recordings are transformed into digital voices for assistive speech devices. This simplicity ensures ease of use and accessibility for users, allowing them to maintain their unique voice and communicate effectively with their loved ones.[3]

We designed Anima V3 to have the least pages needed to incorporate all of our core functionalities. Simplicity in app design is crucial because it enhances usability, reduces development time and costs, and improves user satisfaction.[5]

Learn more about our core functionalities here, and our application design here.
