Project Background

Anima v3 is a type of augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) technology that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate synthesised speech based on someones voice. The system is designed to help individuals with speech impairments, such as those with Motor Neuron Disease (MND) or amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), to communicate more easily. Until now, voice banking has been a challenging, time-consuming, and exhausting process, particularly for individuals facing debilitating medical conditions. Our goal is to provide assistance to those who have received a devastating diagnosis, allowing them to preserve their sense of self by making the voice banking process more enjoyable and easier. This will enable them to continue communicating with their loved ones even after losing their ability to speak.

Anima v3 Goals

What we want to achieve

Our primary objective was to develop an accessible, functional, and user-friendly application that enables users to store their voice while also delivering a meaningful experience. In addition, we aim to bring better dialect & accent & language and make Anima v3 accessile to a wider audience. We have also established ambitious deadlines so that Anima v3 can be deployed and start helping people as soon as possible.

Pain Points:

  • Lack of easy ways to bank your voice
  • Lack of free ways to bank your voice
  • Technical requirements can be hard for people who are not proficient with technology
  • Time-consuming
  • For individuals with physical disabilities or medical conditions, such as ALS, it can be challenging to maintain consistent vocal quality throughout the recording process.

Anima v3 Cures:

  • Makes Voice Banking simple and easy to do
  • Limited technical knowledge needed
  • Emotional, bonding time with loved ones
  • Easy to use
  • Free to use

MoSCoW Requirement List

Summarizing all our findings above, we gathered our MoSCoW requirements list.

Functional List


Priority Requirement Description
Must Haves
  1. We must allow users to record their voice

  2. We must have a voice profile for each recorded voice

  3. We must be able to process a piece of text and play it out loud

  4. We must allow users to type what they want to say

  5. We must support multiple languages

  6. We must have a local file storage

  7. We must have a single backend executable

  8. We must have a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate for users
Should Haves
  1. We should aim to develop software that meets the requirements for the Microsoft Store and is ready for publication.

  2. We should implement support for accents and dialects
Could Haves
  1. We could let users highlight specific parts of text to read out loud
Won't Haves
  1. We won't have an app that uses paid higher-quality TTS engines
